The leader and oldest member of Mamamoo is Solar (born Feb 21, 1991). The youngest member (maknae) of Mamamoo is Hwasa (born Jul 23, 1995). The tallest member of Mamamoo is Moonbyul, with a height of 165 cm (5'5''). The shortest members of Mamamoo are Wheein and Hwasa with a height of 162 cm (5'4''). Wheein did not renew her contract with RBW in 2021 but signed a contract extension to stay as part of Mamamoo until December 2023.
